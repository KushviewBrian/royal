Premium Cigars

Curated Collection

At Royal Smoke & Tobacco, we offer an extensive selection of premium hand-rolled cigars from around the world. Our collection features both well-known brands and exclusive finds that cater to connoisseurs and newcomers alike.

From mild and smooth options perfect for daytime enjoyment to full-bodied, complex cigars for special occasions, our inventory is carefully selected to meet diverse preferences and palates.

Our knowledgeable staff can help you discover new favorites and provide recommendations based on your taste profile and experience level.

Quality & Storage

All our cigars are stored in optimal conditions in our climate-controlled humidor to ensure freshness and quality. We receive regular shipments to maintain a rotating inventory of fresh cigars.

We carry popular brands such as Arturo Fuente, Padron, Macanudo, and Romeo y Julieta, as well as boutique options for those looking to explore unique flavors and limited editions.

Whether you're looking for single cigars or box sets, we offer competitive prices and expert advice to enhance your cigar experience.

Vapes & Vaping Supplies

Latest Technology

Our vape selection features the latest technology in vaping devices, from beginner-friendly pod systems to advanced box mods for experienced users. We stock devices from top manufacturers known for their quality and innovation.

Whether you're transitioning from traditional smoking or looking to upgrade your current setup, our knowledgeable staff can guide you toward the right device for your needs and preferences.

We offer a wide range of accessories including coils, batteries, chargers, and tanks to keep your device performing at its best.

E-Liquids & Flavors

Discover our extensive collection of e-liquids in countless flavors and nicotine strengths. From classic tobacco and menthol options to exotic fruit blends and dessert flavors, we have something for every palate.

We carry reputable brands known for their quality and consistency, ensuring a satisfying and safe vaping experience. Our selection includes both high-VG options for cloud production and high-PG blends for flavor intensity.

Ask our staff for recommendations based on your preferences and device type.

Tobacco Products

Classic Selection

Our tobacco collection features a diverse range of pipe tobacco and rolling tobacco options to suit various tastes and preferences. From aromatic blends to natural, additive-free options, we carry quality products from respected manufacturers.

We stock both domestic and imported tobacco varieties, including classic American blends, English mixtures, and aromatic tobaccos with various flavor profiles. Our selection caters to both casual users and serious enthusiasts.

Regular inventory updates ensure fresh products, and our staff can provide recommendations based on your flavor preferences and smoking style.

Premium Options

For the connoisseur, we offer premium tobacco options including limited editions and specialty blends that are carefully aged for enhanced flavor complexity.

We carry various forms of tobacco including loose-leaf, plug, twist, and ready-to-smoke options. Our selection includes both traditional and modern blends to provide a comprehensive range of choices.

Proper storage and handling of our tobacco products ensure maximum freshness and flavor retention.

Hookah & Accessories

Premium Hookahs

Our hookah selection features quality pieces from respected manufacturers, ranging from traditional designs to modern innovations. We carry options for both beginners and experienced hookah enthusiasts.

From compact, portable hookahs perfect for travel to elaborate pieces for home use, our selection includes various materials, sizes, and styles to match your preferences and needs.

Each hookah is carefully selected for quality construction, smooth operation, and durability.

Flavor Selections

Discover our extensive collection of hookah tobacco (shisha) in numerous flavors and brands. We stock both traditional favorites and innovative new flavors that provide unique experiences.

Our selection includes various flavor profiles from fruit-based options to more complex blends, including sugar-free and herbal alternatives for those seeking different experiences.

We also carry all necessary accessories including coals, hoses, screens, and cleaning supplies to ensure a complete hookah experience.

CBD Products

Wellness Focused

Our CBD product selection focuses on quality, wellness, and transparency. We carry products from reputable manufacturers who provide third-party lab testing to verify potency and purity.

From tinctures and capsules to topicals and edibles, our CBD selection offers various consumption methods to meet individual preferences and wellness goals.

Our knowledgeable staff can provide information about different CBD products and help you select options that align with your wellness objectives.

Quality Assurance

All our CBD products are sourced from legal hemp with less than 0.3% THC, in compliance with federal regulations. We prioritize products made from organically grown hemp when possible.

We maintain detailed information about the sourcing, manufacturing process, and lab results for our CBD products to ensure transparency for our customers.

Whether you're new to CBD or an experienced user, we offer products with various potency levels to meet your individual needs.
